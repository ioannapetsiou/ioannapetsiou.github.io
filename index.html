About Me
I am a data journalist, passionate about transforming data into stories that elevate silenced voices and expose environmental injustice.
I hold a degree in Communication, Media and Culture from Panteion University, where I first discovered my passion for data journalism through a project on rising temperatures in Greece, Spain, Italy, and Portugal. In 2024, I was awarded a fellowship from SNF to attend the Lede Program at Columbia University, where I gained hands-on experience with Python, web scraping, data visualization, and digital storytelling. A few months later, I received a scholarship to join Panteion’s data team at the CSIS Journalism Bootcamp, where we reported on restrictive migration policies.
From Athens to New York to Washington, the projects below reflect my journey through journalism, data, and storytelling.
